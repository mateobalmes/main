Empleo

Company Description

Empleo is a social enterprise that matches disenfranchised jobseekers to blue- and grey-collar employment through analytics.

Highlights

  • Engineered, launched, and improved the web application; outlined product strategy and set best practices for product growth.
  • Facilitated recruitment of 35,000+ jobseekers and enrolment of 40,000+ trade-work aspirants in partnership with 600+ training centers and recruitment firms; managed a total of 45 part-time and full-time team members in the entire time the enterprise was in operation.
  • Secured PHP15M in funding and $80k grant from Facebook
  • Awards: Best Social Impact by the ASEAN Ricebowl Awards and Top 5 ASEAN Impact: Scale Category in 2017

Milestones

  • Hailed winner of the Best Social Impact Award in the Philippines in 2017 by the ASEAN Ricebowl Awards
  • Recognized as 1 of the 5 most impactful Scale stage startups in the 2017 ASEAN Summit in the Philippines
  • Represented the Philippines in the 2019 Taiwan Startup Exhibit
  • Selected to participate in the 2019 Regional YSEALI Program in Myanmar.
  • Recognized as 1 of the Top 10 enterprises in the YE! Community Startup Competition. The team subsequently represented the Philippines in the 2015 YE! Summit for frontier-markets founders in the Netherlands.
  • Won a spot in the Top 10 Business Model Competition by Esquire Financial Philippines.
  • Chosen to take part in the 2019 United States Department of Peace's PeaceTech Spring cohort.
